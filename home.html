3D Arts
Generalist

I am Hendrick I. Gaddi a multimedia artist specializing in 3D Arts and I go as Pootetu on my social media platforms. I am a hard-working 3D artist, specializing in Blender and anime-style NPR models.

With a firm grasp of the unique aesthetics and expressive nature of anime characters, I excel in bringing these characters to life in three-dimensional form. I take pride in being capable of capturing the essence and charm of these styles, whether it's designing original anime-inspired characters or faithfully recreating existing ones.

So, if you're looking for captivating anime-style models and animations, I would love to bring your vision to reality.
